We, at NatWest Markets, want to put Innovation at heart of what we do.

This begins with building a community and providing a platform to discuss, share and build upon ideas that help us to be more efficient in what we do and possibly, explore additional sources of revenue for NWM.

To bring this to play, this website provides a platform for you to submit your ideas & vote ideas submitted by your colleagues. How can you take your idea to something concrete aka launch is explained below as per the framework.

The Framework

From voicing a raw idea to launching it, the following framework will set in place formal review and support mechanisms to get appropriate traction at each stage (gate) through which the idea progresses.

Progress gates explained

The Idea Gate, Your ‘Eureka’ moment!

  • Come up with an idea and gauge the value it creates for the organisation.
  • Pen it down in simple language on the portal and submit it.

The ‘Initial Review’ Gate

  • The innovation community (your peers and colleagues) reviews these ideas and rates them. (This review will take place every 3 weeks, which gives ample time for the innovation community to provide feedback and for you refine your idea).
  • The Innovation Core Group (ICG) reviews the ideas (giving due consideration to the ratings associated with them) and selects some which can be taken forward.
  • ICG informs the individuals associated with selected ideas.

The ‘Pitch’ Gate

  • The idea is vetted further by the senior members of NWM.
  • The idea is clearly articulated, with a thorough cost/benefit analysis.
  • Individuals are allocated to work on the ask (resources) – money, people, etc.
  • Individuals create a final pitch.
  • The Head of NWM Markets, India, SLT + ICG listen to the pitch and provide feedback – Go / No-Go/ Improvise.

The Launch

  • This is where the build process starts.
  • The idea patrons (ICG, SLT) help you with your asks and ensure that you get adequate support.
  • Depending on the idea, a specific time period is allotted to you in order to build upon your idea.
  • Build → Test → Review.

Remember, ideas are a dime a dozen, what really matters is the passion and the focus to follow through. Please proceed to Submit Idea...
